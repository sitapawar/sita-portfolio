About

Get to know me

Sita is a junior studying Computer Science and Comparative Literature at Brown University.

With a focus on visual computing, she loves exploring designs and the technology behind them. Her interest in Comparative Literature means she enjoys narrative and storytelling, with a particularly fondness for Italian Renaissance Epic Poetry, and she wishes that compiling between Javascript and Typescript could count towards translation requirements. Certainly: She loves exploring the intersections across her interests, from considering the narratives present in design and data visualizations to exploring the potential in literary technology and digital humanities.

In her free time, Sita enjoys reading, mountain biking, painting, hiking, and playing Ultimate Frisbee. Her goal is to retire to a small castle in Scotland.
